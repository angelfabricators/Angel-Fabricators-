WHO WE ARE

Engineering Excellence Since 1985

Angel Fabricators Pvt Co Ltd was officially established in 1985 with a vision of delivering reliable engineering, fabrication, and industrial maintenance solutions. Since then, we have built a reputation for quality workmanship, innovation, professionalism, and customer satisfaction.

With more than 40 years of experience, we have successfully completed projects across Tanzania, Kenya, and Zimbabwe, serving mining, manufacturing, agriculture, food processing, and industrial sectors.

Our Mission

To provide world-class engineering, fabrication, and industrial maintenance services through innovation, professionalism, and quality workmanship while creating sustainable employment opportunities for young people and contributing to economic development.

Our Vision

To become one of Africa's leading engineering and fabrication companies recognized for excellence, innovation, integrity, and sustainable industrial solutions.
